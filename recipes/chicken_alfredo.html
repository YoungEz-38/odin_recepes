The chicken Alfredo Process

How a well prepared chiken alfredo looks like...

The astonishing aroma of this chicken
alfredo fills your lungs. As your mouth begins to water
you start think about the different sauces and spices used in in
this magnificent chicken. Suddenly the waiter steps in to ask
the meal tastes. You use this opprtunity to ask what
different ingredients are used in this dish.


The ingredients:

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

How to guide:


  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook for 5-7 minutes per side until fully cooked through. Let rest for 5 minutes, then slice or shred the chicken.
  3. In the same skillet, add the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and let simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Reduce heat to low and whisk in the Parmesan cheese and butter until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  5. Add the cooked fettuccine and shredded chicken to the sauce. Toss to coat everything evenly.
  6. Serve the chicken alfredo immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan if desired. Enjoy!
